About

Practical security, explained clearly.

I am an early-career Cyber Security Consultant with experience delivering cyber consulting, risk management, governance and secure architecture across highly regulated and critical systems.

I have supported the design and assurance of secure systems throughout the product lifecycle, embedding security from the design phase through to implementation and ongoing assurance.

I enjoy breaking down complex cyber challenges through a methodical and analytical approach, transforming them into practical, risk-based solutions that are accessible to both technical and non-technical audiences.

Through my NCSC-certified Cyber Security degree apprenticeship, I have developed the skills to contribute to projects spanning critical national infrastructure, safety-critical systems and operational technology.

I take pride in delivering high-quality work and continually developing both professionally and academically. As I approach the completion of my apprenticeship, I remain committed to expanding my knowledge, taking on new challenges and broadening my experience.
